# Gym Management System

A complete solution for gym owners to manage their business, members, classes, and personal training services.

---

## What It Does

### For Gym Owners

**Set Up Your Business**
- Create your gym business profile during a simple onboarding process
- Add multiple gym locations under one business
- Manage all your locations from a single dashboard

**Manage Your Gyms**
- Add gym details: name, address, contact information
- Set opening hours and list available facilities
- Activate or deactivate locations as needed

---

### For Member Management

**Track Your Members**
- Add and manage gym members
- Store member contact details and emergency contacts
- Assign members to subscription plans
- Track membership start and end dates

**Easy Check-Ins**
- Each member gets a unique QR code
- Quick scan for gym entry
- Track check-in history and attendance patterns

---

### For Classes & Scheduling

**Create Class Types**
- Set up different class types (Yoga, Spin, HIIT, etc.)
- Define class duration and capacity limits
- Add descriptions for members to see

**Schedule Classes**
- Create weekly timetables
- Assign instructors to classes
- Set room/studio locations
- Manage class capacity

**Handle Bookings**
- Members can book into classes
- Track attendance and no-shows
- Waitlist management for full classes

---

### For Personal Training

**Manage Personal Trainers**
- Add trainer profiles with their specialities
- Set trainer availability and rates
- Assign trainers to specific gym locations

**PT Packages**
- Create session packages (e.g., 5 sessions, 10 sessions)
- Set pricing and validity periods
- Track package usage

**Book Sessions**
- Schedule one-on-one training sessions
- Track completed sessions
- Manage cancellations and rescheduling

---

### For Payments & Billing

**Subscription Plans**
- Create different membership tiers (Basic, Premium, etc.)
- Set monthly or annual pricing
- Define what each plan includes

**Track Payments**
- Record member payments
- Generate invoices
- View payment history
- Track outstanding balances

---

## Getting Started

1. **Sign up** with your email or Google account
2. **Create your business** - Enter your gym business name
3. **Add your first gym** - Enter location details
4. **Start managing** - You're ready to add members, classes, and more

---

## Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you'll see:

- **Dashboard** - Quick stats and overview of your gyms
- **Gyms** - Manage your gym locations
- **Members** - View and manage all members
- **Classes** - Set up timetables and manage bookings
- **Personal Trainers** - Manage PT staff and sessions
- **Business** - Your business settings and subscription
